# 01. Product Brief

## Product Name

Tenbagger Check

Korean name: 텐버거 체크

## One-line Description

관심 있는 종목이 장기 성장 후보인지 스스로 점검할 수 있게 도와주는 텐버거 체크리스트 서비스.

## Product Positioning

Tenbagger Check is not a stock recommendation service.

It is an educational self-check tool that helps users review whether a stock idea may be worth further research.

The app does not tell users what to buy or sell.  
It helps users think more carefully before making their own investment decisions.

## Target Users

This product is for:

- Individual investors who are interested in stocks but struggle to evaluate stock ideas.
- People who hear about stocks from YouTube, blogs, news, or social media and want to check the idea before acting.
- Beginner investors who want a simple framework for thinking about growth stocks.
- Users who want to avoid emotional buying based on hype or FOMO.

## User Problem

Many individual investors find it difficult to judge whether a stock idea is actually worth researching.

They often see a stock mentioned online and feel pressure to buy quickly.

Common problems include:

- Buying based on hype without checking basic factors.
- Confusing a popular theme with a strong company.
- Ignoring valuation and entry risk.
- Focusing only on upside while ignoring downside risk.
- Not having a simple checklist for reviewing stock ideas.

## Core Value

The app helps users pause before making an emotional investment decision.

It gives users a simple structure to review:

- Market growth potential
- Company growth potential
- Competitive advantage
- Financial quality
- Entry risk

The goal is to support better thinking, not to predict returns.

## MVP Goal

The first version should allow users to:

1. Enter a stock name or ticker.
2. Answer five checklist questions.
3. Receive a score out of 100.
4. Read a cautious interpretation of the score.
5. See a clear notice that the result is not investment advice.

## MVP Features

### Included in Version 1

- Stock name input
- Five checklist questions
- Simple score calculation
- Result summary
- Strength/risk interpretation
- Not-investment-advice notice

### Excluded from Version 1

- Real-time stock prices
- Financial statement data
- News data
- AI-generated investment analysis
- Buy/sell recommendations
- Price targets
- User accounts
- Saved history
- Payment
- Backend API
- Database
- Charts
- Social sharing

## Scoring Categories

The first version uses five categories:

1. Market size and growth potential
2. Company growth potential
3. Competitive advantage
4. Financial quality
5. Valuation and entry risk

Each category contributes up to 20 points.

Total score: 100 points.

## Score Interpretation

- 80 to 100: Strong candidate for further research, but risks still need careful review.
- 60 to 79: Interesting candidate, but more evidence is needed.
- 40 to 59: Some potential, but the case is not strong enough yet.
- 0 to 39: Weak fit for a tenbagger-style growth candidate.

## Required Disclaimer

The app must clearly show:

"이 서비스는 투자 추천이 아닙니다. 제공되는 점수와 해석은 사용자의 자기 점검을 돕기 위한 참고 정보입니다. 모든 투자 판단과 책임은 투자자 본인에게 있습니다."

## Words to Avoid

Avoid these phrases:

- Buy
- Sell
- Strong buy
- Guaranteed return
- This stock will rise 10x
- Next confirmed tenbagger
- Target price
- Must-buy stock

## Preferred Words

Use these phrases instead:

- Self-check
- Candidate review
- Growth potential checklist
- Risk check
- Research support
- Educational tool
- Not investment advice
